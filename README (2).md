NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Agreement governs the exchange of confidential business information between the two parties identified below.

1. Obligations. The Receiving Party shall not disclose any Confidential Information received from the Disclosing Party to any third party, and shall use such information solely for the purpose of evaluating a potential partnership.

2. Confidential Information. Confidential Information means any business plans, customer lists, pricing information, or internal documents shared between the parties.

3. Parties. For purposes of this Agreement, Brightline Media Group ("Disclosing Party") is the party sharing information, and Marcus Reid ("Receiving Party") is the party receiving it.

4. Survival. The obligations of confidentiality shall continue for so long as the information remains confidential, with no fixed expiration specified.

5. Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Illinois.
